Who We Are

More Than a Hostel.
A Home That Shapes You.

Mettaloka Youth Centre has been a sanctuary of learning and brotherhood since 2003 — quietly shaping the lives of hundreds of young men in Mysuru.

When a student steps into Mettaloka, they don't just find a bed and a meal. They find a structure — a daily rhythm of study, responsibility, and mutual respect that becomes the foundation for the rest of their lives.

We believe that the years after 10th grade are defining ones. With the right environment, the right peers, and the right discipline, any student can unlock their fullest potential. That is what Mettaloka exists to provide.

Admissions

The Selection Process

Joining Mettaloka is a commitment — to yourself, to your studies, and to your brothers.

Selection Criteria

  • 01
    Post 10th Grade

    Open to students who have completed their 10th standard examinations.

  • 02
    Genuine Intent to Study

    A clear, sincere motivation to pursue education in a focused environment.

  • 03
    Willingness to Follow Rules

    Commitment to respecting the code of conduct and contributing to community life.

  • 04
    Carla Home Students

    Special consideration for students from Carla Home background seeking a structured academic path.

Student Life

Discipline is Freedom

A structured daily life that builds independence, responsibility, and focus.

Phone Policy

Degree Students

Smartphones permitted for academic purposes — research, notes, and educational tools only. Responsible usage is expected.

PUC Students

Basic phones only. This ensures full focus on foundational studies during the most critical academic years.

Student Responsibility

At Mettaloka, students are trusted to manage their own affairs — a key part of their growth into responsible adults.

  • ✔ Fees are managed independently by students

  • ✔ Senior students mentor juniors
  • ✔ Conflict resolution within the brotherhood
Programs

Life Beyond Textbooks

A rich calendar of events that nurture the whole person.

🎙️ Every Sunday

Sunday Seminars

Weekly gatherings where students engage with speakers, discuss ideas, and broaden their perspectives beyond the classroom. A cornerstone of the Mettaloka intellectual culture.

🌍 Regular

Community Service

Students actively participate in community outreach and social service activities, nurturing empathy, humility, and a sense of responsibility toward society.

🏏 Seasonal

MPL – Mettaloka Premier League

Our in-house sports tournament that brings together all centers in the spirit of healthy competition, teamwork, and celebration. The MPL is one of the most anticipated events of the year.

📋 Monthly

Monthly Meetings

Regular structured meetings where students voice concerns, share updates, plan ahead, and collectively strengthen the brotherhood. A democratic space where every voice matters.
