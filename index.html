Who We Are

More Than a Hostel.
A Home That Shapes You.

Mettaloka Youth Centre has been a quiet pillar of the Mysuru community since 2003 — providing a home, a structure, and a brotherhood to young men stepping forward after Carla Home.

When a student walks into Mettaloka, they carry the values already instilled at Mahabodhi — meditation, discipline, character. Here, those values meet the demands of higher education. Students find not just accommodation and meals, but a living framework that makes it easier to focus, grow, and become who they are meant to be.

We believe the years after 10th standard are defining. The environment you live in shapes the person you become. That is what Mettaloka is built to be — the right environment.

Our Roots

Mahabodhi Carla Students Home

Mettaloka is exclusively for students who have passed their 10th standard from Mahabodhi Carla Students Home, Mysuru. This is not an open hostel — it is a continuation of the same journey.

180Children from across India
8Dormitories & House Fathers
2Monks for spiritual guidance
17Support staff

Mahabodhi Carla Students Home is home to 180 children hailing from different parts of India. Two monks provide spiritual guidance through daily chanting and meditation — practised every morning and evening — along with intensive Dhamma classes and meditation courses during vacations.

The home places its strongest emphasis on character building and moral education. Students develop creativity through special summer camps, extracurricular activities, and handicrafts — drawing, painting, cooking, gardening, and music — building great confidence that stays with them through life.

Eight House Fathers serve as wardens across eight dormitories, and 17 additional staff ensure every child is nourished, cared for, and given the best foundation for their future.

How Students Join Mettaloka

01
Complete 10th at Carla Home

Only students who have finished 10th standard at Mahabodhi Carla Students Home, Mysuru are considered for Mettaloka.

02
Character-Based Selection

Students are chosen based on their values, commitment to discipline, and readiness for higher education in Mysuru.

03
No Open Applications

Mettaloka does not accept outside applications. There is no open admission process. Selection happens entirely through Mahabodhi Carla Home.

04
A Continuation, Not a New Start

Joining Mettaloka is not a new beginning — it is a continuation of the path of Dhamma, discipline, and brotherhood that started at Carla Home.

☸️ Mettaloka is a closed, values-based community — not a commercial hostel. All residents share the same roots at Mahabodhi.

Phone Policy

Degree Students

Smartphones are permitted for academic purposes — research, notes, and digital study tools. Responsible usage is expected at all times.

PUC Students

Basic phones only — ensuring complete focus during the most critical years of foundational education.

📌 Phone use is a matter of trust. Misuse affects the whole community, not just the individual.
💼

Responsibility

Students at Mettaloka are treated as responsible adults. Managing their own affairs is part of the journey.

  • ✔Fees managed independently by each student
  • ✔Self-discipline upheld during study hours
  • ✔Active contribution to community upkeep
  • ✔Senior students mentor and support juniors
  • ✔Conflicts resolved within the brotherhood
  • ✔Punctuality and attendance self-managed

About This Community

Mettaloka Youth Centre is not an open hostel. We do not accept outside applications or requests for accommodation from the general public.

All residents are selected from students who have completed their 10th standard at Mahabodhi Carla Students Home, Mysuru. Selection is based on character, readiness, and a shared commitment to values.

If you are a student or parent from Carla Home and have questions, please reach out via phone or email — we are happy to speak with you.
