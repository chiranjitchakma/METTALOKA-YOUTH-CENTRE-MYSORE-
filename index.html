Who We Are

More Than a Hostel.
A Home That Shapes You.

Mettaloka Youth Centre has been a quiet pillar of Mysuru since 2003 — providing a home, a structure, and a brotherhood to young men stepping forward after Carla Home.

When a student walks into Mettaloka, they carry the values already instilled at Mahabodhi — meditation, discipline, character. Here, those values meet the demands of higher education. Students find not just accommodation and meals, but a living framework that helps them focus, grow, and become.

We believe the years after 10th standard are defining. The environment you live in shapes the person you become. That is what Mettaloka is built to be — the right environment.

Our Roots

Mahabodhi Carla Students Home

Mettaloka is exclusively for students who have passed their 10th standard from Mahabodhi Carla Students Home, Mysuru. This is not an open hostel — it is a continuation of the same journey.

180Children from across India
8Dormitories & House Fathers
2Monks — spiritual guidance
17Support staff members

Mahabodhi Carla Students Home is home to 180 children hailing from different parts of India. Two monks provide spiritual guidance through daily chanting and meditation — practised every morning and evening — along with intensive Dhamma classes and meditation courses during vacations.

The home places its strongest emphasis on character building and moral education. Students develop creativity through summer camps, extracurricular activities, and handicrafts — drawing, painting, cooking, gardening, and music — building confidence that stays with them through life.

Eight House Fathers serve as wardens across eight dormitories, and 17 additional staff ensure every child is nourished, cared for, and given the best foundation for their future.

How Students Join Mettaloka

01
Complete 10th at Carla Home

Only students who have finished 10th standard at Mahabodhi Carla Students Home, Mysuru are considered for Mettaloka.

02
Character-Based Selection

Students are chosen based on values, commitment to discipline, and readiness for higher education in Mysuru.

03
No Open Applications

Mettaloka does not accept outside applications. There is no public admission process — selection happens entirely through Carla Home.

04
A Continuation, Not a New Start

Joining Mettaloka is a continuation of the path of Dhamma, discipline, and brotherhood that began at Carla Home.

☸️ Mettaloka is a closed, values-based community — not a commercial hostel. All residents share the same roots at Mahabodhi.

Programs

Life Beyond the Classroom

A rich calendar of activities that nurture the whole person — not just the student.

🎙️Every Sunday

Sunday Seminars

Weekly gatherings where students engage with ideas, hear from speakers, and develop perspectives beyond the classroom — a cornerstone of Mettaloka culture since the beginning.

🌍Regular

Community Service

Students actively engage in outreach and social service — nurturing empathy, humility, and responsibility toward the broader community, rooted in Dhamma values. All community service activities are carried out in association with Mahabodhi Carla Students Home, Mysuru. Our students participate wholeheartedly and lend their hands to every initiative — whether it is cleaning drives, support activities, or special events — continuing the same spirit of seva they grew up with at Carla Home.

🏏Seasonal

MPL – Mettaloka Premier League

The in-house sports tournament uniting both centres in healthy competition. The MPL is one of the most anticipated and celebrated events of the year. Ex-students actively contribute to and participate in the football tournament — bringing their experience, spirit, and brotherhood back to Mettaloka and inspiring the current students to compete, grow, and celebrate together.

📋Monthly

Monthly Meetings

Structured gatherings where students voice concerns, plan ahead, and collectively strengthen the community — a democratic space where every voice genuinely matters.
